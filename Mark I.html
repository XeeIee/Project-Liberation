DATA: Mark I is the very first successful project in regards to The Bullseye Project, however by all means is still a prototype of future models.

However the model was never ready for mass production and The Insurgency wanted to find a way to test it's current capabilities. And as if 'god' answered you showed up at the front gate to give Mark I a test drive.

Mark I's mass production intended to make them multi-purpose tools of warfare and mass destruction, luckily the one you fought was merely a prototype and was missing it's highly durable outer shell. Strategy:
* Mark I follows a very strict battle protocol, you'd better know it as an attack pattern. This pattern changes after every 3rd of their health disappears, so keep an eye out for a potential attck style that you can exploit.

* Mark I classifies as a brute, if you attack them 5 times, they'll be angered and prone to take downs. when their health is high, it just deals a chunk of damage, if their health is low enough, however, that's one easy kill.
