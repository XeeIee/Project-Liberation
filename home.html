Tip of the chapter:

There's a difference between Critical attacks and Heavy attacks. Learn the difference!
A CRIT is a random occurance, it can happen with any attack, at any point in time.
A HEAVY Attack is when you charge an attack, this can only be done with Melee and Unarmed attacks, unfortunately not ranged. Heavy attacks are also less about physical damage (in most cases) and more about dealing effects to an enemy or a teammate, be cautious!
