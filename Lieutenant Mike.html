DATA:

Mike used to be part of the Infiltrations Department for The Agency, before being moved to serve Alice in her own division. The two made a fond bond and they became good friends. Mike has personally swore to keep Alice safe under all circumstances, even to the point of sacrifice or betrayal.

Ofcourse during the raid that The Insurgency had staged on their outpost Mike found himself with heavy and near fatal injuries that affected his ability to serve. despite The Agency trying to give him a medical discharge, he insisted that he would continue to serve under Commander Alice. after a long debate, he succeeded and recovered overtime all while watching over her.

He was willing to help in her ambitions for Project Liberation and had helped in the preparations in doing so, where Alice was the brains, he would be the Brawns, in terms of planning anyways. Although he had not fully healed to his best and couldn't dodge he used to, he could still pack a punch and that's all that mattered to him to keep Alice safe.

However it may take a while for him to get used to looking after a whole squad like Alice does.


Strategy:
* Mike Is an aggressive person, Trying to attack where he sees fit. while he's lacking in overall firepower, he does carry flashbangs on him, read up on flashbangs in the weapons category to find out about them.

* Mike came prepared for the worst, and expected some of you to get downed. The only Solution? bringing some adrenaline syringes! He has brought 2 just in case, as well as Alice, don't let them go to waste.

* Mike is a horrible dodger ever since the incident, and has to rely on tanking or making heavy dives which leave him vulnerable, keep an eye out to see what he's doing just as much as you would with Alice in case you need to support him, he may return the favour at a later date.
