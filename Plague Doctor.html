Plague Doctor
Arena Boss

DATA: DIFFICULTY SCALE - 1

The Plague Doctor is an interesting case. Originating from Tower Defense X, he is one of the first bosses you could encounter in that game. We like to use him often because of how easy he is to defeat. sure he may have massive health pools but his gimmicks do not work in this sort of game. Which is why he's here!


Strategy:
* The Plague Doctor's Primary focus is debuffing. he achieves this with his spray gun. It's important to know what colour will do what to your team if hit by it. Be mindful that Gas Masks will nullify these effects.
Blue Spray - Affects Dodge chance and other speed related stats
Green Spray - Affects Attack potency and other attack chance stats
Red Spray - Affects general damage
If you know which one's which, then you can easily take him out with co-ordination. Better yet, now you know which ones to use if you end up taking his stuff!
