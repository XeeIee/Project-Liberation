The Interloper
Creator of The Cell - Leader of The Interlopers

DATA: The Interloper is an Omnipresent entity with unlimited scale in powers and capabilities. The motives of The Interloper are not easy to understand by anyone, however what is understood is that it used its abilities to create The Cell, a HyperVerse that houses a wide plethora of timelines, realities, universes, multiverses, and omniverses in scale order. An area in particular is closed off from the rest of the HyperVerse to contain all of the Campaigns that currently exist, will exist or were going to exist. This includes in no particular order: The Potential Life, The Deformed Land, False World, SOULS, Games Collide, Now Or Never, Protectors of the Core, Project Liberation, Tales of the Depths, Another Roleplay, The Degens, and War of the Witless. All of these Primary universes surround the Nexus Point, the core that makes up the field that contains all these universes.

Basically Zero information is known about the origins or creation of the Interloper, the same applies to how much it can do. Though, since the creation of The Cell the Interloper has used its powers to enforce and protect universes via direct intervention from outside anomalies that jeopardise the course of a universes timeline. It used to directly and physically intervene, but now instead of that it works behind the scenes to ensure balance, as too much physical interaction will break a universe. Instead of this, The Interloper has a select few individuals, Also with the title of Interlopers, to do the work across The Cell for it. these people are also referred to as Interlopers, and are in the billions, which isn't many in the scale of Infinity. And only the most trusted of Interlopers are allowed within the range of the Nexus Point. Current Known Interlopers: Xeelee, Assistant, Kel_SP(Terminated)
(If you would like to enlist a character to also hold the status of Interloper, DM me)

These Interlopers have a portion of Interlopers abilities, allowing them to Shapeshift, manifest, modify, and warp, amongst other things, this is all possible with a secondary creation known as the Console, a coding console that allows users to manipulate reality with commands and scripts as if the world was all lines of code.

The Interloper initially stumbled across The Potential Life, an idea and concept created by one Slipstream Gold. From this, it liked the idea and wanted to try and experiment with a creation of their own. The result was a Branch off of the potential life, Known by you as Project Liberation. But something went wrong along the creation, more specifically, Something broke through the field that wasn't supposed to, this entity that broke through is from a race known outside of The Cell as The Shattered. Their name? Calidus. It didn't matter how they got in, their presence was not to be ignored, it would leave Project Liberation willingly, and could destroy it and the other Campaigns. And so, it started it's story early, to lead the events of Project Liberation, the Liberation now being to keep the grasp of this world off of Calidus.
