DATA: Riker Soarum, Brother to Razor Soarum, was a member of the second batch of The Saving Grace. Excellent with a bow and arrow, and an amazing detective, He was one of the forefront Supports for his team, able to draw to conclusion with an incredibly fast and accurate wit, and draw a shot as fast as those conclusions came. A deadly archer, to be feared by many who crossed him.

But of course, due to the TPL Incident, which he was ultimately apart of, The Soarum Family mainly fell apart. Razor is MIA, his cowardice and bearing of the Impact Gauntlet making him a forever target for The Insurgency. And Of course Riker being executed as part of a "no witnesses" rule when trying to execute Maks Maladroit. The Insurgency had an initial hypothesis that The Razor Clones all only came in gradients of blue and green, so when the MERC Scientist had heard of a Black Pumpkin dispatched by their mercenary squad, he knew he had struck some form of gold.

Riker's body was first experimented on with a computer augment that basically scooped out a part of his head to fit a small computer like machine. This was enough to stimulate the body into a state of respiration. He was then dragged to the mining sector, put through all sorts of processes, but was especially steered clear of the parasites, as they were still trying to figure out how to maximise their results working on it. The MERC Scientist was able to secure a red gauntlet from Calidus' collection, for hefty prices of course. Most of his innards were scooped out, replaced with metal internals and artificial augments. He felt the whole process. And for some reason, they decided to alienate his name, changing him from Riker, to RiX0r.

Behind the subservience caused by a lack of control over his actions, RiX0r can still express his true self in the form of NotePad files that he writes using the computer augment he was given at the start of his transformation. Though overtime these files slowly became more and more incomprehensible as he lost himself more and more to agony, lack of control, and time.

And in most cases, Razor would never know what happened to him. It's sickening how we may never know what happens to a loved one, especially when we're not looking.

Strategy:
* The Red Gauntlet is the weakest known gauntlet, only having a Harpoon like grapple system with an incredibly strong pull and exceptionally strong rope. Each Gauntlet has the capabilities to keep a host alive through exceptional means, requiring severe damage to the head or the torso in order to dispatch them. or at the very least severing the arm. Where this would normally kill a Gauntlet host, It won't work on RiX0r due to his vast augments making the body move without a life system needed. Don't think this will be an easy task regardless.

* The SpearHead has been primarily used as a mobility tool by RiX0r, moving him great distances away from trouble. However, don't get caught by the Head yourself, as it is incredibly hard to pry it out of yourself without losing good amounts of health. The best way is to pry the wound open and the head out with a sharp object, and knock the harpoon back to RiX0r. The increased speed of which the harpoon will fling towards him should tear his decaying body, stunning him for a turn.

* RiX0r's behavior is very predictable. He likes to ambush, so keep an eye out around corners or through doors if you suspect he's nearby. In combat, he works on a simple "Offence, Defence" System. Backing up and keeping distance while protecting himself if you play aggressively, and Pushing and giving you little breathing room if you play cautiously. It's best to balance when to go offensive or defensive, but it's incredibly important to slow him down. Depending on the mode depends on whether he is more likely to hit and dodge attacks. Offensive = more hits, Defensive = more dodges.
