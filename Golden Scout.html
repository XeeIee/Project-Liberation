DATA:

Before collaborating with The Insurgency, The MERCS, better known currently as the Matter and Energy Reclamation Corporation, were Notorious for their mechanics, being able to build very complex and in depth machinery, weapons, armour, and anything inbetween. They would supply these items to Factions across the United States, and upon moving to the UK to expand their influence, The Insurgency took hold and how keeps the UK HQ of MERC Within' their walls of the took over city.

Nobody knows how this was pulled off, for many it just seemed to happen over night, however the reason lies within Golden Scout, the closest thing the MERCS have to a leader, and one that supposedly killed the MERC's old leader, and also an agent that worked with The Insurgency to betray the MERC's and put them under the work of The Insurgency.

The MERC's now specialise in twisted experiments, however remnents of their old creations still exist, as well as new ones, in fact, most if not all of the machines that you encounter belong to the MERCS, minus 1 or 2 exceptions.

Despite Golden Scouts snarky bravado, once challenged, he often breaks his tough persona in change for a more cowardice like one. He is a hard to understand individual, and even his rival, Commander Alice, sees him as an absolute nutjob, yet somehow he is the most convincing lier many have seen, being able to even bullshit his way through the leader of The Insurgency, not only does a feat like that take massive guts to accomplish, but it also requires massive wit and a capability to withstand the leader's twisted nature.


Strategy:
* Golden Scout fights dirty, however he is also cowardly, most odds he will try to run away from you once the game's not in his favour, so try to keep him around if you don't want him running off like the coward he is.

* Golden Scout is a really tough opponent, and is your first heavy obstacle in your way of completing Project Liberation, it would be wise to not under estimate, regardless of your situation, he loves surprises.

* Golden Scout has a plethora of weapons and will try to Use his full arsenal against you. One of his weapons is a Flashbang, Which if thrown will stun the majority of your. The best way to counter act this deadly move is by throwing the Flashbang back, which will in turn stun him instead, however don't expect him to be out for long, nor should you expect him to go into a dizzy state.
