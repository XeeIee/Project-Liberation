Abomination
Supreme Husk

DATA: The Abomination is a freak against nature, a metalic interior shell mostly empty aside from the skeletal replications that make up it's foundations, and a fleshy Exterior, pooling out of a long decayed suit of robotic armour. The Shell of the armour that protected it has long since decayed, however in it's full structure it was used as the protective layer for Mark II. Due to the priorities and importance of The Abominations existence, and paired with the impeccable design of Mark II's detachable armour, the armour was given to The Abomination.

The Abominations properties, however, made the flesh begin to absorb the armour into the flesh. Which did increase the durability of the flesh, but also left a hideous mass in place of the neat design of the armour. This did result in the Flesh being unreasonably tough, to the point that The Abomination is considered indestructable by many. In reality, it's just incredibly hard to destroy.
