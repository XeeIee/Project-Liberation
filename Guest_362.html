Guest_362
Supreme MERC

DATA: Guest_362, like Guest_360, is a supreme member of the faction he supports. MERC Don't actually have many people that are specifically committed to MERC, many of them are hired mercenaries or people with temporary contracts, Guest_362 is one of those Mercenaries, Joint with Molten and GS as a trio in the earlier days, but after Molten became Light, and GS became the leader of MERC, 362 decided to extend an extra helping hand by extending his contract.

Guest_362 currently sits as the Left Hand Man of MERC, one of the best engineers the world has seen, while also being a 'certified' Medic due to his own infamous item, the Regen Coil. This is actually a modified version that acts as a far less dangerous Medical Accelerator.

Guest_362 and 360 are both friends, however the reasons why are a bit more vauge, as 362 reminds 360 of another guest, 361, however it's unclear why. It's also unclear why there are a string of people all named Guest with the numbers 360 and beyond. Every guest, 360, 361, 362, 363, 364, and 365 are rumoured to actually be all parts of one person, split into separate similar bodies and personalities, that's the conclusion The Insurgency came up with anyways, remains to actually be seen. There are also also rumours of a more mysterious and evil 359, but nobody, not even the other guests, can confirm his existance.

So far the only ones that are apart of The Insurgency and MERC alike are 360 and 362, there are rumours of another guest to somehow be involved, but nobody has ever gotten to confirm this.

The Line of Guests is a deep mystery, one that only the Guests themselves fully understand.

Strategy:
* Almost very Guest can be reasoned with, they aren't morally bad people and are usually actually pretty chill, once their health is low enough, they become reasonable, which will allow you to try and diplomatically converse with them, assuming no other enemies are nearby.

* Like Engineers, Guest_362 will often pilot machinery in his fights to garner the upper hand, whether they be robots, mechs or vehicles, they will still be used, best that you look for weak spots within the machines (If you were able to recover phineus before hand, then this is a cakewalk.)

* Guest_362's regen coil isn't as impactful as you think, only healing by small margins every turn, but if you really want to stop him from doing that, then any stun esc effects can temporarily stop the healing.
